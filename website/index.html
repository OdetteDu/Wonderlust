Boredom Happens.
Wonderlust can save the day.

We’ve all been there: waiting in line, on the bus, in the bathroom. You’re effectively stuck, and you have only your phone as a source of entertaining yourself. You’ve checked your email. You’ve returned some texts. You’ve thumbed through your Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, and news feeds. You’ve consumed every morsel of fresh data your phone is able to serve up. But you’re still stuck, and now, bored.

Enter Wonderlust.

Stop Wasting Time.
Start Wondering.

Instead of scrolling through Facebook, learn two minutes' worth of trivia about samurai, about the six degrees of Kevin Bacon, about Natural Selection, about the Sorites Paradox, about escargot. Pick fields you wish to explore and let Wonderlust gather knowledge that might spark your interest into bite-sized chunks that satisfy your craving for fresh information and indulge your intellectual curiosity at the same time. Accumulate a basic familiarity with a broad range of ideas and topics to recover lost minutes in a way that is fun, interesting, productive, and painless.
